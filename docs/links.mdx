## AO3 Links

- [AO3 Collection](https://archiveofourown.org/collections/thmprompts/profile)<br /><span className="subNote">main
  collection page. read the prompt fills or see prompts here.</span>
- [See Open Prompts](https://archiveofourown.org/collections/thmprompts/requests)<br /><span className="subNote">check
  our open prompts for writing or art.</span>
- [AutoAO3 Prompts List](https://autoao3app.firebaseapp.com/#/thmprompts/all)<br /><span className="subNote">a
  better formatted version of the open prompts above.</span>
- [Add Your Prompts](https://ao3.org/collections/thmprompts/signups/new)<br /><span className="subNote">add
  your prompts for someone to fill.</span>
- [How to Add/Edit a Prompt Guide (Detailed)](https://bit.ly/3bSsok7)<br /><span className="subNote">detailed
  instructions for adding prompts in case AO3 throws you for a loop.</span>
